~Tidy for Turtles~

Oh no! The beach is littered with trash.
Help Mr. King, a computer science teacher who wears a pink shirt, clean it up before it harms Trevor the baby sea turtle and all his turtley cool friends.
But tread carefully! If you step on any turtles, the species will go extinct.
